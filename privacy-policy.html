Privacy Policy

App Name: Project Tracker  |  Effective Date: April 19, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 19, 2026

1. Personal Information We Collect

Project Tracker may collect the following information to provide its services.

  • Email address (optional): collected when linking an account for multi-device sync
  • App usage data: project names, update history, improvement items, notes, and other content directly entered by the user
  • Anonymous user identifier: a temporary ID automatically created when using the app without logging in (stored on the device)

2. Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information

  • To store and sync project data
  • To provide data sharing across multiple devices
  • To improve service quality

3. Third-Party Service (Firebase)

This app uses Google Firebase services.

  • Firebase Authentication: user authentication (anonymous and email-based)
  • Firebase Firestore: data storage and synchronization

You can review Firebase’s Privacy Policy here:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

  • Email account users: until an account deletion request is made
  • Anonymous users: local data is deleted when the app is deleted, and Firebase server data is automatically deleted after 90 days

5. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

This app does not sell collected personal information or provide it to third parties. However, Firebase (Google) processes data as a service provider for app operation.
